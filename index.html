About
Society for Soybean Research and Development (SSRD)

The Society for Soybean Research and Development (SSRD) is a registered society with its headquarters located at the ICAR-Indian Institute of Soybean Research, Khandwa Road, Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India.

Objectives of the Society:

  • ✅ Promoting Scientific Interaction: Encouraging interaction and fellowship among soybean researchers.
  • ✅ Publishing Knowledge: Publishing an English journal titled "Soybean Research", reports, and newsletters.
  • ✅ Organizing Events: Hosting seminars, symposia, and workshops for discussions on soybean production.
  • ✅ Encouraging Research: Promoting interdisciplinary R&D programs relevant to soybean production.
  • ✅ Supporting Initiatives: Undertaking initiatives aligned with SSRD’s mission.
